USER

Users are individuals or entities who rely on online services, websites, or web applications. They are the end consumers of these services.

Role in DDoS Attacks:

Users are often the first to notice the effects of a DDoS attack when they experience service disruptions, slow response times, or complete unavailability. DDoS attacks can impact users by preventing them from accessing essential services, causing frustration, and potentially leading to lost revenue or productivity.

Responsibilities:

 Report unusual service behavior to administrators or customer support if they suspect a DDoS attack. Implement preventive measures on their own systems or networks if they manage them.
